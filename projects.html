Firepit with Friends

I have always loved the firepits I would see on backyard renovation shows. After a lot of research, I finally found one that would be big enough to seat many friends for our gatherings and still be cozy.

Materials needed:

  • (6) 3" x 3" x 7' posts (legs)
  • (6) 3" x 3" x 5' posts (supports)
  • (6) 1" x 1" x 5' posts (design-top)
  • Box of screws
  • Black matte paint
  • 5 wooden swings with chains and hooks
  • Gravel
  • Paver stone for fireplace
  • Firepit kit
